About

He goes by the name of Riley Writtens,a hip hop/rap artist, born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. He Grew up around lyricists, especially a cousin from a rap group called Pitch Black. Inspired by House Party and 8 Mile, he wrote his 16 at 16 yrs old and Hasn't looked back since. He's performed at Mike Geffner's The Inspired Word, End Of The Weak, DJ Kay Slay's Showcase & featured acts at Justice En El Barrio, Bar Fest at Nuyorican Lounge & Roc The Mic at Amarchi Lounge. He's dropped four projects: Unwritten Mixtape, The Writual, 7 Capital Cities To Heaven: Vol. 1 & FIF.
